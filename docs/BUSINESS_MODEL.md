# Business Model

## Vision

The premium dating layer for on-chain natives. Wallet = identity, on-chain reputation = trust, crypto = checkout. Launch on the Solana Seeker dApp Store to reach crypto-native users with higher revenue retention than traditional app stores.

---

## Target market

| Segment | Profile | Why Grap3 |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| **Primary** | Seeker owners + Solana natives | Underserved by mainstream apps; value wallet verification |
| **Secondary** | Crypto Twitter / multi-chain users | Cross-pollination via events + quests |
| **Tertiary** | Protocol communities | Sponsored discovery pools (B2B) |

---

## Value proposition

1. **Verified humans** — wallet history + activity reduces bots without phone/email gate
2. **Status that matters** — trust score, Seeker badge, NFT-gated pools
3. **Native checkout** — USDC/SOL, no app-store tax
4. **Privacy-first** — start with pubkey only; add PII when user chooses

---

## Monetization

### Subscription tiers

| Tier | Price | Core perks |
|------|-------|------------|
| **Free** | $0 | 10 likes/day, basic discovery, trust score visible |
| **Plus** | $7.99/mo | Unlimited likes, see who liked you, 1 boost/week |
| **Gold** | $14.99/mo | Plus + priority stack, 3 super likes/day, travel mode |
| **Seeker Pass** | Included w/ device | Free monthly boost, +10 trust bonus, exclusive pools |

### Grapes (soft currency)

- **1 Grape = $0.10** — psychological pricing layer for micro-transactions
- **Packs:** 10 ($1), 50 ($4.50), 100 ($8)
- **Spend on:** Super like (5 Grapes), Boost (25 Grapes)
- **Earn via:** Daily login streak, event check-in, partner quests

### À la carte (USDC/SOL direct)

| Feature | Price |
|---------|-------|
| Super like | $0.50 |
| Boost (30 min) | $2.50 |
| Verify badge (SBT) | $5 one-time |

### B2B & events

| Stream | Model |
|--------|-------|
| **IRL mixers** | Ticketed crypto singles events + sponsor booths |
| **Protocol quests** | Protocols pay for sponsored pools |
| **Brand partnerships** | Seeker ecosystem co-marketing |

---

## Competitive moat

1. **First native dating dApp on Seeker** — distribution advantage
2. **Crypto-native payments** — margin structure incumbents can't match
3. **Open-source core** — community trust + contributor pipeline
4. **On-chain trust graph** — compounding data advantage
5. **Grapes + events** — engagement loop beyond swipes

---

## Risks & mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Cold-start (no matches) | City-by-city seeding, event IRL, bot-free niche |
| Trust & safety | Moderation API, report/block, age gate 18+ |
| Crypto downturn | USDC pricing; Grapes reduce sticker shock |
| Regulatory | No token; plain payments; legal review pre-launch |
| Privacy (GDPR) | Minimal PII, data export/delete endpoints |
