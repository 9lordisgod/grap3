# Go-To-Market

## Beachhead

Seeker owners — small, concentrated, crypto-native, reachable. Win this niche first.

## Launch sequence

1. Seeker dApp Store launch + perks for Seeker device holders
2. Crypto community campaign ("find your on-chain match")
3. City-by-city seeding to solve liquidity
4. IRL crypto-singles mixers at major conferences

## Growth loops

- Referral rewards: free Boosts / Premium days (both sides)
- Verified-badge social flex shared on X
- Partner quests: protocols sponsor "date + earn" campaigns → new users

Not "crypto Tinder" — "the dating app where your on-chain reputation matters and bots can't get in."
